Contact
=========================================
- Bug reports
  
  Please report all problems and bugs on the  `GitHub Issues page <https://github.com/wistaria/MateriAppsInstaller>`_ . To resolve bugs early, follow these guidelines when reporting:

  - Please specify the version of Materiapps Installer you are using.

  - If there are problems for installation, please inform us about your operating system and the compiler.

  - If a problem occurs during execution, enter the input file used for execution and its output.

- Contact

  If you have any questions about topics related to your research that are difficult to consult at Issues on GitHub, please contact the following contact information.

  E-mail: mainstaller-dev_at_issp.u-tokyo.ac.jp (replace _at_ by @)
